Hello! I'm a college freshman with a passion for computer science and cultural studies. From a young age, I've had a passion for computers and a captivation for the endless possibilities offered by computer science itself. I've also fostered an understanding of why representation in STEM is so important. I believe that seeing powerful women that look like us in positions we aspire to, is vital for young women of color. It allows us to envision ourselves in these positions while instilling the confidence needed to reach our goals. This is the motivation behind my endeavors and the reason why I dedicate time to helping young women such as myself find resources that will assist them in reaching their goals and volunteering with organizations that are dedicated to the betterment of youth.
